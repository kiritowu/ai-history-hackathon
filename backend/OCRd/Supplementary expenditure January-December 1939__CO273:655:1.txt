The paved area at the head of the slipway and in front of the Hangars is of concrete slabs laid loosely on the earth filling of which the Civil Airport is made.

When the Empire class Imperial Airways Flying boats are towed up to the hangar from the seaplane channel by a caterpillar tractor, the loose slabs tilt on their bases, owing to the heavy weight of the aircraft, in such a manner that the Chief Engineer of Imperial Airways, Ltd. considers it is unsafe to continue to bring the aircraft ashore.

2. Simultaneously with the execution of the Settlement work involved, the Settlement Officer has prepared rough registers in which have been entered particulars of the Crown Title (if any), or the old Dutch or other Titles and subsequent conveyances, affecting each lot.

3. 4. This application is to enable the necessary Registers and Forms to be purchased.

23 FISHERIES, S.S. & F.M.S.—

OTHER CHARGES

SPECIAL EXPENDITURE

Purchase of a motor vessel to replace the motor cruiser, "Talang"

Provision made in the Estimates—Nil.

This provision is required to purchase a motor vessel to replace the motor cruiser, "Talang" at the Northern Districts (Kuala Kurau). The cruiser has been in commission since 1923 and is now worn out and requires extensive overhaul and repairs estimated at $5,500.

2. This expenditure was not anticipated last year and was not, therefore, included in the current year's estimates as it was then expected that the motor cruiser, "Talang" would continue to be serviceable until 1940. Unfortunately, it has been found that she would not last till then.

3. As a motor vessel is necessary in order to enable the Fishery Officer to perform his duties at sea, collecting fisheries revenue and inspecting Bagans, fishing villages, etc., which are only accessible by sea, it will not, therefore, be possible to postpone this expenditure until next year without interfering with the public service.

4. Savings from the other items of the estimates to offset this expenditure are not available.

5. Half of this expenditure is recoverable from Federated Malay States.
